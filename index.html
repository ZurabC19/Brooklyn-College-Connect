About the Website

BC Connect is a centralized platform to showcase all BC events — from academic workshops to club socials and sports meets.

It’s designed for students, faculty, and staff to stay in sync with what's happening around campus.

With powerful search, filters, and RSVP features, it helps boost event visibility and community involvement.
